Virelabs

Develop of an image detection system for recognising wheelchairs and strollers. With a camera placed inside a tram, when the tram doors close, a still-image is taken. The still image is then processed with Yolo(v3) and Darknet locally by using "Virebox". Trained a deep neural network with Yolo to detect those objects (wheelchairs and strollers). For that it was neccesary to download few hundred of images from Google images with Commercial Creative Commons license and annotate them by using "labelimg" software. For generating new images it was used Gimp for manipulating images by placing wheelchairs and strollers in them. The work was divided in teams and done task and working hours were reported to the Project Manager in a weekly basis.
