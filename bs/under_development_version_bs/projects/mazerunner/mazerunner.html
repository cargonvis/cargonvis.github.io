Maze Runner

Company: None     Working time: May 2021 - XXXX 2022

 

Assembly and coding a functional robot. Pieces were individually bought and assembled (chassis, motors, wheels, sensors, depth camera, jetson nano, arduino, motor driver boards), and code was made for avoiding obstacles and mapping any kind of scenary where the robot may be located. Also neural network was trained for detecting fire sources and the robot being able to extinguish it with a small fan. Project made in simulation and real life by using ROS and Gazebo in Ubuntu 18.04LTS.
