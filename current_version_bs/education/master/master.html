M.Sc. in Robotics and Embedded AI

Duration: September 2024 - Present

Institution: Maynooth University, Ireland

Field of Study: Electronics and Computing Science

Specialization: Robotics, Artificial Intelligence and Embedded Systems

Description:

Upon starting my master's at Maynooth University, I built upon my foundational knowledge from my undergraduate studies, delving deeper into the realms of robotics and embedded AI. The program is designed to bridge computing, electronics, and mechanical engineering, equipping me with the specialized skills necessary to design and control intelligent machines.

The curriculum included courses in core areas such as Reinforcement Learning, Machine Learning & Neural Networks, Artificial Intelligence & Language Processing, and Deep Learning. These courses advanced my understanding of AI, particularly in its application to robotics. My learning extended to practical topics like Introduction to Control Theory for Robotics and Autonomous Mobile Robotics, where I explored the principles underlying robot movement and autonomy.

I also focused on the intersection of robotics and human interaction through courses like Human-Robot Interaction and 3D Vision and Augmented Reality. These modules provided insights into the ethical and practical challenges of designing robots that can seamlessly integrate into human environments.

The program emphasized teamwork and innovation, with a major team project each semester. In the first semester, my team and I concentrated on robot design and innovation, applying design thinking methodologies. The second semester’s project shifted towards system realization and evaluation, allowing us to bring our designs to life and test their functionality in real-world scenarios.

My master’s thesis will be a culmination of these experiences, conducted in partnership with either an industry leader or within Maynooth’s robotics research group. This project will demonstrate my ability to apply advanced AI techniques to solve complex problems in robotics, solidifying my expertise in embedded AI systems.

Upon graduation, I will have gained not only deep technical knowledge in robotics and AI but also invaluable experience in innovation, teamwork, and the practical application of cutting-edge research in real-world scenarios.
