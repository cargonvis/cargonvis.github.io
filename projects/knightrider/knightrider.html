Customized Virtual Assistant in Embedded Systems

Company: None     Working time: October 2021 - January 2022

 

Development of a customized virtual assistant with the voice of KITT, the car in the Knight Rider TV show from the 80's. Coded in Python in a Raspberry Pi. With touch screen for a better user experience. Also added some visuals with an arduino nano board that activates some leds lights depending in the Hz of the voice.
